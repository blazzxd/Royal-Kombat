Royal Kombat

8888 Unique NFTs

THE STORY

Designed as digital memorabilia, Royal Kombat is a collection of unique characters inspired by the global combat scene. With a total of 8888 uniquely randomized NFTs, it is the first Romanian project of its kind and has the purpose to create an online community for those passionate about combat sports.

THE BENEFITS

Ownership includes commercial and creative rights and gives you access to the community, creating global networking opportunities both online and offline, granting exclusive VIP access to key local and global fighting galas and enabling you to take part in multiple giveaways uncovered along the way.

THE ACADEMY

The project aims to support young talent. From the very early stage of graphic design, we have created paid internship opportunities for students from the Bucharest National University of Arts. Once the project reaches 50% of sales, share of the profit will be donated to support various youth combat schools from different cities in Romania.
